About Me

Aloha! Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Zachary Higa, and I am currently a graduate student pursuing a Master's Degree in Statistical Science at the University of Idaho. I am seeking an internship role with a position as a data analyst, statistician, or data scientist.

As a person with an innate curiosity and love for observing certain trends in the real-world, I have always held an appreciation for numerical data. Whether our data of interest is the field goal percentages of numerous NBA teams or an update on the percentage of people vaccinated for COVID-19 throughout the U.S., every set of numerical data has their own story to tell. Additionally, the stories that are held within these data sets may not only be fascinating, but they comment on trends that are observed in the real-world. Thus, I aspire to pursue a career as a statistician who is able to tell meaningful stories of real-world data for others to be able to understand.

With more exposure to various datasets and more knowledge accumulated through my courses, I learned the usefulness in developing algorithms and visualizations from given data to tell these stories. Therefore, I wish to apply these skills that I gained as a data analyst or data scientist.
