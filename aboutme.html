About Me

Aloha! Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Zachary Higa, and I am currently an IT Data Analyst with the Office of the Vice President for the University of Hawaii Community Colleges (OVPCC). I have been with OVPCC since November 2023 where I have further developed my analytical skills through various projects. My daily responsibilities have included the maintenance and development of new Tableau dashboards, pulling data from our system's Operational Data Store (ODS) and our Banner Student Information System (SIS) with Oracle SQL and PostgreSQL, and putting together presentations with data visualizations and data storytelling for our key stakeholders.

In addition to my professional experience, I graduated with a Masters of Science in Statistical Science at the University of Idaho in Spring 2024, and I graduated with Bachelors of Science in Mathematics from the University of Hawaii at Manoa in Spring 2021. After graduating with my degree in mathematics, I knew that I wanted to transition into a more applied mathematics field rather than in theoretical mathematics, and I had an interest in being able to tell stories through data. Thus, I enrolled in my Masters degree program that led me on the path to becoming a data analyst.

I hope to further develop my skills as a data analyst while also learning more about incorporating machine learning and artificial intelligence into my workflow. In this rapidly changing world with so many technological innovations, there is always something new to learn and improve the efficiency of my work.
