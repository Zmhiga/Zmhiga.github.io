Analysis of UHCC English 100 Placement Dashboard

The UHCC English 100 Placement Dashboard was released on Tableau public in January 2024 to look at student pass rate data by placement measure. This dashboard was intended to be viewed by English instructors and counselors throughout the UH Community College system, but the dashboard can be daunting for people jumping into the data blind. Therefore, though I did not participate in creating the dashboard, I prepared slide decks that were presented to any of the interested UHCC personnel to help in navigating through the dashboard and providing some insights that can be found through the data present in the dashboard. The general slide deck that was presented to English instructors and counselors during an informational Zoom session is included below.

Analysis of EdReady English as a Placement Measure

The EdReady test was created as an online placement measure in response to the Covid-19 pandemic. Since the EdReady placement test is an unproctored, online placement test, a few English staff throughout the University of Hawaii Community College have wanted to see this placement measure discontinued as we shift away from the pandemic period. However, most of the data collected shows that the EdReady placement exam is a solid predictor of success in the English 100 course throughout the UHCC system. Therefore, the slide deck presented below was aimed towards the UHCC personnel who wanted to see the discontinuation of EdReady English as a placement measure.
