Community College Survey of Student Engagement (CCSSE) Administration

Led the survey administration of the Community College Survey of Student Engagement (CCSSE) during the 2024 academic year for the seven UHCC campuses. I was responsible for coordinating the campus leads to ensure that the administration of the survey will run smoothly.

In preparation for the survey administration in the Spring of 2024, I worked with the former UH learning management system, Laulima's, IT team to include a pop-up message to students when they logged on to Laulima to access their courses. Below is what the pop-up message looked like for one of our campuses:


In addition to working with Laulima IT, I worked with OVPCC's marketing director to send the emails and reminders to students to complete their survey.

During the survey administration, I wrote a code in R to automate the process of providing a weekly update on the students who had not yet completed the survey. I sent this list of students to the CCSSE campus leads for them to leave targeted marketing efforts to the students who have yet to complete the survey. The R Code that I used is attached below.



After the administration of the survey, I wrote a random number generator code in R to randomly select 40 of the survey completers to receive an Amazon gift card. Additionally, I worked with the OVPCC fiscal office to figure out the necessary documentation (WH-1 form and Prize Acknowledgement form) needed to procure the gift cards for the students.
