UHCC Data Dashboards

As an IT Data analyst at OVPCC, I maintain the following four dashboards in our UHCC data site. The four dashboards that I am responsible for maintaining are: Credit Courses, Continuing Education, Financial Aid, and Performance Indicators dashboard.

Credit Courses Dashboard

The Credit Courses dashboard contains enrollment data, academic data, and demographic data of students in our UHCC credit programs. Adhering to the DOE regulations, we suppress small cell sizes for categories where the denominator is smaller than 10 and the numerator is smaller than 5. The dashboard pulls from UH's ODS via Oracle SQL.

However, in October, there will be a migration to Banner Baseline 9 and ODS will be replaced by Ellucian Insights. With this migration, I am in the process of converting the Oracle SQL script to PostgreSQL since the Ellucian Insights platform will pull from a PostgreSQL database. Additionally, this new PostgreSQL script will pull from Banner tables rather than ODS tables since the ODS tables will not reside in the UH system tenant in Insights.

Continuing Education Dashboard

Similar to the Credit Courses dashboard, the Continuing Education dashboard contains enrollment data and demographic data of students in our UHCC non-credit programs and adheres to small cell size suppression. However, instead of pulling from ODS, the dashboard pulls from a Non-Credit perpetual data source in Tableau Cloud that is connected to our Destiny database (where most of our UHCC non-credit data resides).

Though most of our non-credit data resides in Destiny, Honolulu CC apprenticeship and Hawaii CC ESOL are two programs that are in our Banner SIS. Because of this, I created a new data source in Tableau Cloud that connects to Banner and pulls the apprenticeship and ESOL data via Oracle SQL. After that, I created a Tableau Cloud flow that will union the Destiny data source and Banner data source to create a comprehensive data source that includes all non-credit program data. Then I loaded this new data source to the Continuing Education Dashboard.

Financial Aid Dashboard

The Financial Aid dashboard was originally created to provide a high-level overview of financial aid to administrators in the UHCC system. From this dashboard, you can look at the distribution of federal, state, institutional, and external aid given to students in the past aid years.
