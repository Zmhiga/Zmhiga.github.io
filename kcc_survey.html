Kapiolani CC Wellness Survey Analysis

With inspiration from a prior Tableau dashboard I was charged to analyze the data from a Kapiolani Community College employee wellness survey done in Fall 2023. From the prior year, they transitioned from a Google Form survey to SurveyMonkey. I was asked to provide a Tableau dashboard similar to what was done for the prior year's analysis and incorporate some comparison with last year's survey data to see if there was any changes and improvements.
I was provided with the raw survey data, and the first thing I did was parse through the responses to redact any identifiers that were included in the responses. Then the some of the data needed to be transformed in Tableau Prep before loading into Tableau to get the data in a usable format. Namely, this transformation was needed for the sections on Employee concerns, commendations and challenges of Kapiolani CC's processes, plans, and policies.
From there, I loaded four data sources into Tableau (the raw survey data and three transformed data sets). I created the banner and some of the headers in the dashboard in Canva. I visualized most of the data similarly to the dashboard from the prior year's survey to try to maintain consistency, though the commendations and challenges sections were new, and these sections allow for the user to hover over each of the bubbles to view the specific survey responses that were linked with the feelings listed. Additionally, in the concerns section, I added a comparison to last year's results in the tooltips, so the user can see how their concern level regarding a certain area improved or did not improve.
